**Problem Statement**

Analyze e-commerce sales data to identify trends, customer behavior, and key factors impacting revenue and performance.

**Tools Used**
	•	SQL
	•	Power BI

**Dataset**

E-commerce dataset sourced from Kaggle.
Due to large file size, the dataset is not included in this repository.

**Key Analysis**
	•	Analyzed overall sales and revenue trends
	•	Identified top-performing products and categories
	•	Evaluated customer purchase behavior
	•	Examined regional performance
	•	Created KPIs for revenue and order metrics
  
**Key Insights**
- The United Kingdom generated the highest revenue.
- Seasonal spikes were observed during holiday months.
- A small percentage of customers contributed to a large portion of revenue.
