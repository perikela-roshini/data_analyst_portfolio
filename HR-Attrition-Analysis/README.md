📌 HR Attrition Analysis

📖 Problem Statement

Analyze employee attrition and identify key factors driving employee turnover within the organizatio

🛠️ Tools Used
	•	SQL
	•	Power BI

📊 Dataset

IBM HR Analytics dataset from Kaggle containing employee demographics, job roles, and performance details.

🔍 Key Analysis
	•	Calculated overall attrition rate
	•	Analyzed attrition by department and job role
	•	Evaluated impact of salary and overtime
	•	Examined job satisfaction and work-life balance

💡 Key Insights
	•	Overall attrition rate is ~16%.
	•	Sales Representatives have highest attrition (~39%), driven by low income.
	•	Sales (~20.6%) and HR (~19%) are the most affected departments.
	•	Attrition is driven by salary(Sales) and overtime 
  - Attrition is driven by Job satisfaction and Work-life balance (HR)
